Installation Guide for WooCommerce Phone Verification by RingCaptcha

Plugin Name: WooCommerce Phone Verification by RingCaptcha

Version: 2.3

Author: RingCaptcha

Description: WooCommerce Phone Verification by “RingCaptcha” is a WooCommerce Plugin that enables you to verify whether a user phone is real or not. All you need to do is install the plug-in, and register at RingCaptcha to get your App key and Secret key and you’re done!

Installation and Setup

  1. Download "WooCommerce Phone Verification by RingCaptcha".
  2. Exact the .zip file. It contains 3 folders: documentation, licensing, and ringcaptcha2woocommerce.
  3. Locate the folder name "ringcaptcha2woocommerce" and compress to .zip file.
  4. Go to your WordPress Dashboard » Plugins » Add New » Upload » Select "ringcaptcha2woocommerce.zip", click "Open".
  5. Next, Click "Install Now"
  6. Click "Activate Plugin".
  7. After Activation, Go to WooCommerce Dashboard » RC2wooCommerce Dashboard.
  8. Go to RingCaptcha and register to get your App Key and Secret Key.
  9. After Register, login to your account and create a new app. Under "My Apps" tab, click "Create New".
  10. A window will popup. Choose "Web" as App type.
  11. Input your website’s domain name. "Localhost" is accepted for development purpose. Click "Create App".
  12. You can now view your newly created app from the dashboard. Now you can see your App Key and Secret Key.
    (Note: If you see **** in Secret Key, just click it and it would reveal your key).
  13. Copy and paste the App Key and Secret Key on your WooCommerce RingCaptcha Settings. Also check the checkbox to enable RingCaptcha2wooCommerce. Click "Save Changes".
  14. Congratulations! Now you can use the difference functionalities of the WooCommerce RingCaptcha plugin!
